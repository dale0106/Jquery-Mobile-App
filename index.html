Step 1 of 7

Turn on your mac

Press the power button on your mac located at the top right corner of your computer

Step 2 of 7

Turn your wifi ON

  • Once your mac has booted, locate the wifi symbol on the top right hand corner of the screen and click it
  • select "Turn Wi-Fi On"
  • Exit the window
Back Next
:)

Step 3 of 7

Open System Preferences

  • Locate the apple logo at the top left hand corner of the screen and click on it
  • Select System Preferences from the drop down menu
Back Next
:)

Step 4 of 7

Select the Network icon

Once the window has loaded, locate the Network icon and select it.

Back Next

Step 5 of 7

Choose network

  • Make sure you are in the Wi-Fi panel
  • Beside the "Network Name:" option, select the name of your desired network

Enter password

  • If prompted, enter a password
